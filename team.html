Helen Lee
Chief Executive Officer
Helen has been interested in the environment ever since her volunteering experience with Grassroots Ecology. Her experience at Grassroots Ecology involved her removing invasive plants and replanting native plants. This experience motivated her to found Pick-Your-Plant which is a non-profit website dedicated to educating the general public about invasive plants.
