Ox-Eye Daisy
Description:
Ox-eye daisies can grow up to 80 centimeters and has flowers with white petals that surround yellow disc florets.
Leaf Shape:
Pinnately Lobed
Has Thorns:
No
Place to Find:
Bay Area, California
Effect on Ecosystem:
Ox-eye daisies spread very quickly so they outcompete native plants for resources and they also do not have any natural predators.
