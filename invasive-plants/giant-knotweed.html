Giant Knotweed
Description:
Giant knotweed has very large leaves. It also has white flowers.
Leaf Shape:
Reniform
Has Thorns:
No
Place to Find:
Bay Area, California
Effect on Ecosystem:
Giant knotweed has no natural predators so it outcompetes native plants.
