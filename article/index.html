California Invasive Species Action Week
The 9th California Invasive Species Action Week is from June 4 to June 12. There are many volunteer opportunities during this event such as volunteering to remove ivy from Trinidad State Beach, removing foxgloves from Codornices Creek, and removing broom from the American River Parkway.
New Plant Species Being Considered for Protection by California Government
The wildflower known as the Lime Ridge Woollystar is currently being considered to be under the California Endangered Species Act after Christopher McCarron petitioned the California government to protect it because its current habitat is very limited. As of March 4th, harming the plant is illegal.
